Interactive Thread Art

This cursor effect transforms your mouse into a thread weaving tool. Add anchor points by clicking, then watch as colorful threads dynamically connect between these points and your cursor.

Every movement creates new patterns, allowing you to create beautiful geometric string art with just a few clicks and cursor movements.

Creative Potential

Choose from multiple thread colors and adjust thread density to create your own unique thread art compositions. The possibilities are endless!

Create star patterns, spirals, geometric designs or abstract forms - perfect for relaxation, creative expression, or generating unique digital art.

User Controls

Click Canvas

Click anywhere on the canvas to add an anchor pin for your threads.

Mouse Movement

Move your cursor to dynamically create thread connections between pins and your cursor position.

Change Color

Select different thread colors from the palette to change the appearance of your thread art.

Double Click

Double-click anywhere on the canvas to create a symmetrical pattern of pins.
